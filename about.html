Our Story

The lakeside restaurant in Vevey is a renowned establishment that has stood since 1962. Over the years, it has earned a reputation for its refined cuisine and breathtaking views of Lake Geneva. The restaurant is distinguished by its inventive menu, which changes with the seasons, and its exceptional wine cellar. The staff is welcoming and professional, and the atmosphere is warm and elegant. For over 50 years, this restaurant has been a must-visit for food connoisseurs and lovers of beautiful scenery, and is known for its upscale and luxurious dining experience.

Team

The team at the lakeside restaurant in Vevey is comprised of highly skilled and dedicated individuals. The head chef is a renowned culinary artist who is passionate about creating innovative dishes using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients. The service staff is attentive and knowledgeable, ensuring that each guest has a memorable dining experience. The sommelier expertly pairs wines with the menu, offering guests a truly elevated culinary experience. The team is dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience, and it shows in the impeccable service and attention to detail that they provide.
